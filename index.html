Bio plastic cups

Product details

Our compostable cups are an eco-friendly alternative to conventional plastic cups.
Our takeaway cups are certified industrially compostable and carbon neutral.
This range of disposable cups includes compostable clear cups and compostable coffee cups.



About bio plastic cups

The bioplastic cups are made of compostable bioplastic, based on vegetable starch, called PLA.
The eco-friendly disposable cups are strong, flexible, and shatter-proof.
There are also matching lids, plastic-free straws, and inserts available.
